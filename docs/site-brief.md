# Site Brief

## Positioning

This website should feel clear, technical, elegant, and personal without becoming crowded.

The central challenge is breadth: Pablo works across science, software, apps, writing, and analytical consulting. The structure should make that range feel coherent rather than fragmented.

## Visual Direction

Design goals:

- editorial headline typography
- technical body typography
- restrained color palette
- sharp spacing and hierarchy
- minimal chrome
- cards that can scale to many disciplines without visual noise

## Homepage Structure

1. Hero
   Introduce Pablo in one sentence spanning research, software, and writing.
2. Focus areas
   A compact overview of the main branches.
3. Selected work
   Mixed featured items from science, software, and products.
4. Research shelf
   Publication cards inspired by journal/article tiles.
5. Software and labs
   Repositories, internal tools, and in-progress products.
6. Writing and literature
   Novels, short fiction, essays, and science writing.
7. Consulting
   Technical diligence and analytical work.
8. Resume
   Web-native summary with PDF downloads.
9. Reading Room
   A 4x5 catalog grid for book and article recommendations.

## Content Strategy

The site should not try to flatten everything into one bio. Instead:

- research gets publication-style cards
- software gets compact product/repo cards
- writing gets a quieter literary treatment
- consulting gets a more direct, trust-oriented presentation
- the reading section acts as a signal of taste and intellectual context
